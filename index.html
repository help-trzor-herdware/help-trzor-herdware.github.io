How to Get Started with Trezor.io/start

Step 1: Unbox Your Trezor Wallet

Start by unboxing your Trezor hardware wallet. Inside, you'll find the Trezor device, a USB cable, recovery seed cards, and a quick start guide. Ensure you have all the components before proceeding.

Step 2: Connect to Your Computer

Using the provided USB cable, connect your Trezor device to your computer. Ensure that the connection is secure and your device is powered on.

Step 3: Visit Trezor.io/start

Open your web browser and go to Trezor.io/start. This official Trezor webpage will guide you through the initial setup steps. Verify that you are on the correct website to avoid phishing attempts.

Step 4: Install Trezor Bridge

To facilitate communication between your Trezor device and computer, you'll need to install Trezor Bridge. Follow the on-screen instructions to download and install the appropriate version for your operating system.

Step 5: Initialize Your Trezor Wallet

Create a New Wallet

Once Trezor Bridge is installed, choose the option to create a new wallet. Trezor will generate a unique recovery seed consisting of 24 words. This seed is crucial for recovering your wallet if your device is lost or damaged. Write down the seed on the provided cards and store them in a safe place.
